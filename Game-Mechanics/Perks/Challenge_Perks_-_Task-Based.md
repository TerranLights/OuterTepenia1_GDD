# Challenge Perks (Task-Based)

| Perk Name              | Unlock Condition                                      | Ranks | Effect |
|------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------|-------|--------|
| **Blackout Survivor**  | Survive X natural blackouts (10/25/50)                | 3     | +Resistance to blackout effects; better scavenging during them. |
| **Node Deployer**      | Deploy X decentralized power nodes (5/15/30)          | 3     | Bonus reputation with independent/frontier factions; easier hidden path progression. |
| **Crisis Ripple Master** | Exploit X district power ripples (5/15/30)         | 3     | Improved prediction and creative use of energy crises (strong synergy with hidden paths). |
| **Data Archaeologist** | Recover X pre-war or suppressed data archives         | 3     | Bonus to lore knowledge and hidden research paths. |
| **Faction Balancer**   | Maintain positive/neutral reputation with X opposing districts/factions simultaneously | 2 | Reduced Infamy gain when playing both sides or staying neutral. |
| **Ossuary Resonance**  | Spend significant time in Goth/Ossuary areas or complete related tasks | 2 | Unique dialogue and bonuses with Goth faction/religion. |
| **Sonic Attunement**   | Interact deeply with Cymatic Resonance district or sonic tech | 2 | Bonuses to cultural/performative interactions and sonic-based mechanics. |
