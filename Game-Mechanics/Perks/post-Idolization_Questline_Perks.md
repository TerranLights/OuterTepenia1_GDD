# District Capstone Questlines & Signature Perks

**Design Goal:** Each questline is a major, complex, late-game storyline that serves as a thematic capstone for deep investment in that district. All tie into the broader energy crisis and district identity. Reputation system allows "Wild Child" (Idolized + Vilified) outcomes if the player makes controversial choices.

###### (Questline titles and details tentative to possible change and adjustment)

---

## 1. Cancer (Nurturing / Protective – Sanctuary District)

**Questline:** “The Weight We Carry”

**Overview:** A massive wave of severely traumatized Frostlands refugees overwhelms the sanctuaries. An underground “shadow caregiver” network is radicalizing people into total isolation.

**Key Stages:**
- Manage the immediate humanitarian crisis.
- Investigate and interact with the shadow network.
- Resolve a major emotional breakdown among long-term caregivers.
- Decide the future philosophy of care in the district.

**Final Choices & Perks:**
- **Integrate & Reform the shadow network** → **“No One Left Behind”**  
  Strong companion loyalty and revival bonuses. Allies gain significant combat and defensive buffs when near you.
- **Enforce strict official oversight and disband the shadow network** → **“Sanctuary Warden”**  
  Powerful protective aura that reduces damage to nearby allies and grants strong resistance to psychological/trauma effects.

---

## 2. Taurus (Residential / Stability – Home District)

**Questline:** “The Walls We Build”

**Overview:** Old-timers and newcomers are on the verge of violent conflict over housing and resources during a prolonged blackout.

**Key Stages:**
- Mediate rising tensions between established residents and recent arrivals.
- Repair critical heating and farming infrastructure.
- Uncover deliberate sabotage deepening the divide.
- Decide the long-term housing and resource philosophy.

**Final Choices & Perks:**
- **Reinforce traditional gated communities and conservation** → **“Rooted Legacy”**  
  Massive bonuses to your personal home/base (storage, defense, resource generation) and strong buffs when defending residential areas.
- **Force greater integration and shared resource systems** → **“Unbreakable Hearth”**  
  Strong defensive and endurance bonuses when fighting to protect homes or bonded people.

---

## 3. Leo (Cultural / Performative – Morale District)

**Questline:** “The Light We Choose”

**Overview:** During an extended blackout, traditional performers clash with radical newcomers over the future of public art and morale events.

**Key Stages:**
- Organize emergency morale festivals.
- Navigate rivalry and possible sabotage.
- Direct the district’s grand city-wide performance.

**Final Choices & Perks:**
- **Preserve and elevate traditional forms** → **“Light Against the Long Night”**  
  Perform morale-boosting spectacles that temporarily boost ally combat effectiveness and reduce fear/psychological effects.
- **Embrace radical new forms of expression** → **“Spotlight Sovereign”**  
  Strong social and distraction abilities. Draw enemy attention or create powerful diversions through performance.

---

## 4. Scorpio (Psychological / Transformative – Shadow District)

**Questline:** “What We Become”

**Overview:** A dangerous rebirth cult is causing real harm while official clinics have become too rigid.

**Key Stages:**
- Investigate both the cult and official system.
- Confront ethical dilemmas of transformation.
- Handle a catastrophic failed rebirth procedure.
- Decide the future of transformation practices.

**Final Choices & Perks:**
- **Reform while keeping controlled radical methods** → **“Reborn Shadow”**  
  Powerful personal transformation abilities, temporary power surges, and strong resistance to death/trauma effects.
- **Enforce strict ethical oversight** → **“Truth Unveiled”**  
  Major bonuses to detecting lies, uncovering secrets, and resisting psychological manipulation.

---

## 5. Aries (Raw Survival / Energy – Power Core)

**Questline:** “The Price of Fire”

**Overview:** The Power Core is nearing catastrophic failure. Workers are split between maximum output and safety reforms.

**Key Stages:**
- Contain a major instability event.
- Navigate worker and political tensions.
- Make critical safety vs. output decisions.
- Decide the long-term philosophy of the Core.

**Final Choices & Perks:**
- **Push for maximum output with controlled risk** → **“Core Overclock”**  
  Temporary massive boosts to damage or abilities (with some risk).
- **Prioritize long-term stability and worker safety** → **“Guardian of the Flame”**  
  Strong defensive and endurance perks when protecting infrastructure or during blackouts.

---

## 6. Capricorn (Industrial / Ambition – Builder District)

**Questline:** “What We Leave Behind”

**Overview:** A city-saving infrastructure project is threatened by sabotage and worker disillusionment.

**Key Stages:**
- Get the project back on track.
- Investigate sabotage and grievances.
- Decide hierarchy vs. worker empowerment.

**Final Choices & Perks:**
- **Reinforce meritocratic hierarchy** → **“Legacy Builder”**  
  Major bonuses to crafting, construction, and long-term projects. Creations are more durable and efficient.
- **Give workers more voice and shared ownership** → **“Iron Will”**  
  Strong endurance and mental resilience when leading large-scale efforts.

---

## 7. Aquarius (Visionary / Experimental – Innovation District)

**Questline:** “The Edge of Tomorrow”

**Overview:** A high-risk prototype energy project has gone dangerously wrong.

**Key Stages:**
- Contain the prototype failure.
- Navigate radical vs. cautious researchers.
- Make the final call on the project’s future.

**Final Choices & Perks:**
- **Push the prototype to completion despite risks** → **“Prototype Pioneer”**  
  Access to unique experimental abilities and strong bonuses to invention and unconventional problem-solving.
- **Scale back for safer, ethical innovation** → **“Visionary Equilibrium”**  
  Innovation perks that reduce downsides while retaining high potential.

---

## 8. Libra (Diplomatic / Balance – Governance District)

**Questline:** “The Price of Peace”

**Overview:** A major city-wide resource treaty is collapsing, with districts threatening secession.

**Key Stages:**
- Mediate between fracturing factions.
- Uncover hidden agendas and betrayals.
- Forge (or force) a new agreement.

**Final Choices & Perks:**
- **Broker a balanced but imperfect compromise** → **“Arbiter’s Balance”**  
  Powerful negotiation and reputation management abilities.
- **Force a stronger centralized solution** → **“Unshakable Neutrality”**  
  Strong defensive social perks and resistance to manipulation.

---

## 9. Gemini (Information / Communication – Data Nexus)

**Questline:** “The Web We Weave”

**Overview:** A coordinated misinformation and data corruption attack threatens city coordination.

**Key Stages:**
- Trace the source of corruption.
- Decide surveillance vs. information freedom.
- Resolve the crisis and shape the Arcanet’s future.

**Final Choices & Perks:**
- **Implement stronger centralized controls** → **“Arcanet Sovereign”**  
  Major bonuses to hacking, data analysis, and information warfare.
- **Prioritize openness and decentralized systems** → **“Truth Anchor”**  
  Strong resistance to misinformation and powerful truth-revealing abilities.

---

## 10. Pisces (Black Market / Underworld – Shadow Economy)

**Questline:** “The Price of Freedom”

**Overview:** A major power struggle in the black market threatens to spill violence into the city.

**Key Stages:**
- Navigate the criminal underworld.
- Decide whether to dismantle or reform a dangerous syndicate.
- Resolve a major incident caused by the conflict.

**Final Choices & Perks:**
- **Dismantle dangerous elements and add structure** → **“Shadow Broker”**  
  Powerful access to black market goods, smuggling, and unique underworld abilities.
- **Allow more freedom while steering away from harm** → **“Veil Walker”**  
  Strong stealth, deception, and boundary-manipulation abilities.

---

## 11. Sagittarius (Frontier / Exploration – Frostlands)

**Questline:** “Beyond the Ice”

**Overview:** A major discovery/threat in the deep Frostlands requires a large expedition.

**Key Stages:**
- Lead or support the expedition.
- Navigate city oversight vs. frontier autonomy.
- Decide the future relationship between frontier and city.

**Final Choices & Perks:**
- **Strengthen ties while expanding frontier capabilities** → **“Frost Warden”**  
  Excellent cold resistance, exploration, and survival bonuses.
- **Grant the frontier greater autonomy** → **“Horizon Seeker”**  
  Powerful movement, scouting, and discovery abilities.

---

## 12. Virgo (Maintenance / Undergrid – Infrastructure Backbone)

**Questline:** “What Holds Us Together”

**Overview:** A critical city-wide Undergrid failure is imminent and maintenance crews are collapsing from exhaustion.

**Key Stages:**
- Diagnose and contain failing systems.
- Decide between quick fixes and long-term overhaul.
- Address worker burnout and resentment.

**Final Choices & Perks:**
- **Implement aggressive short-term fixes** → **“System Sovereign”**  
  Major bonuses to repair, maintenance, and system optimization.
- **Commit to long-term sustainable overhaul** → **“Silent Guardian”**  
  Strong defensive and endurance perks focused on protecting critical infrastructure.

---

**Design Notes:**
- All questlines are designed to feel like worthy rewards for reaching Idolized status.
- Player choices have lasting consequences on district reputation, city politics, and ending slides.
- Many of these can intersect with the main story’s energy crisis and the final Neutral District climax.
