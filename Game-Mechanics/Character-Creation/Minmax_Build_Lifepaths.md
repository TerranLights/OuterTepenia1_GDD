# Minmax Build Titles & Lifepath Archetypes

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Purpose**: These titles serve as flavorful **lifepath-style archetypes** for characters with extreme stat distributions (three stats at 8+ or three stats at 3 or below). They can be used as background labels, reputation handles, NPC reactions, character sheet flavor, or achievement names.  
**Numbering**: Matches folder structure in `Storyline/Minmax-Builds/`.
