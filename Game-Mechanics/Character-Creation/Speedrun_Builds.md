### Speedrun Builds (Main Quest under 1 Hour)

These builds are designed purely around **MACHINE stats** and **Traits** chosen at character creation.  
**No perks or skills required.**

They enable dedicated players to complete the main quest (stabilizing the power grid + making final distribution decisions) in under 1 hour, with optimized routes pushing toward **sub-20 minutes** and theoretical god-runs in the **8–15 minute** range.

#### 1. The Shortcut Runner (Best for Pure Speed)
**Recommended MACHINE Stats**: High **Agility (A)** + High **Investigation (I)** + Solid **M** or **Calculation (C)**

**Why it works**:
- Exceptional movement speed and shortcut discovery through Undergrid, condemned zones, and risky jumps.
- Quickly spots environmental sequence breaks and hidden paths.

**Ideal Traits**:
- Movement / exploration traits (increased speed, reduced stamina drain, fall resistance, better interactable highlighting).
- Risk-tolerant traits that reward dangerous routing.

**Expected Clear Time**: Sub-15–20 minutes (strongest candidate for world-record runs).

---

#### 2. The Technical Bypasser (Best for Tech-Heavy Paths)
**Recommended MACHINE Stats**: High **Calculation (C)** + High **Engine (E)** + Good **M**

**Why it works**:
- Rapid understanding and manipulation of grid systems, data cores, and stabilization nodes.
- Excellent for rushing “Power Source” and “Governance & Control” chapters.

**Ideal Traits**:
- Technical aptitude traits (faster grid interactions, reduced failure on risky overrides).
- Quick decision-making or machinery affinity traits.

**Expected Clear Time**: Sub-25–35 minutes (especially strong on Technocratic Renewal or Radical Reclamation paths).

---

#### 3. The Explorer / Scout (Best Flexible Route)
**Recommended MACHINE Stats**: High **Agility (A)** + High **Investigation (I)** + High **Nerve (N)**

**Why it works**:
- Superior map awareness and ability to find optimal paths between modular chapters.
- Great at skipping districts via Frostlands edges or abandoned infrastructure.

**Ideal Traits**:
- Exploration and survival traits (better cold resistance, hidden route detection, reduced penalties in hostile areas).

**Expected Clear Time**: Sub-20–30 minutes (very consistent with good routing knowledge).

---

#### 4. The Balanced Decision Maker (Best for Hybrid / Diplomatic Routes)
**Recommended MACHINE Stats**: Balanced high **Calculation (C)** + **Humanity (H)** + **Investigation (I)**

**Why it works**:
- Fast, informed decisions during Distribution Philosophy and Governance chapters.
- Reduces unnecessary backtracking for information.

**Ideal Traits**:
- Dialogue / decision speed traits, reduced faction hostility, or multi-stat affinity.

**Expected Clear Time**: Sub-30–45 minutes (reliable all-rounder).
