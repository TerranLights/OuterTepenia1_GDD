## 2. 4-High + 3-Low Compatibility Charts

Since there are only **7 stats total**, any choice of **4 stats to be High** automatically makes the **remaining 3 Low**.

**Total Valid 4-High + 3-Low Builds**: **35** (one for each choice of the 4 high stats).

### Character Type Summary

| # of High Stats | # of Low Stats | Character Archetype | Description |
|-----------------|----------------|---------------------|-----------|
| **4 High**      | **3 Low**      | **Extreme Specialist** | A highly focused character who is exceptionally powerful in four areas but has glaring, exploitable weaknesses in three others. Often plays as a "glass cannon", "fragile genius", or "one-trick god" depending on which stats are high/low. |

### Example Character Types (Selected Builds)

| High Stats              | Low Stats       | Character Type                  | Playstyle Flavor |
|-------------------------|-----------------|---------------------------------|------------------|
| M + A + C + E           | H + I + N       | **Lightning Engineer**          | Extremely fast, strong, and mechanically gifted, but socially tone-deaf and mentally fragile |
| M + H + I + N           | A + C + E       | **Fearless Empath**             | Physically strong and morally courageous, but slow, bad at planning, and mechanically inept |
| A + C + I + E           | M + H + N       | **Phantom Analyst**             | Incredibly fast, smart, and perceptive, but physically weak and emotionally fragile |
| C + H + I + N           | M + A + E       | **Insightful Philosopher**      | Brilliant, empathetic, and mentally resilient, but physically weak and clumsy |
| M + A + N + E           | C + H + I       | **Unbreakable Courier**         | Physically dominant and fearless, but socially oblivious and bad at long-term planning |

---

### Full Compatibility Rule

- **Any** 4 stats can be set to High (8+).
- The remaining **3 stats automatically become Low** (3 or below).
- There are **no invalid combinations** when doing 4-High + 3-Low.
- You can still mix these with other systems (e.g. titles, lifepaths, or further min-maxing), as long as the stat values don’t conflict.
