# District Armor, Augmentations & Protection Systems

**File:** `District_Armor_Augmentations_and_Protection.md`
**Location:** `Worldspace/Locations-and-Levels/Concordia-City/`
**Date:** June 23, 2026

**Core Philosophy:**
In *Inner Tepenia*, the majority of the player character’s protection comes from their **robotic chassis** and installed **augmentations**, which are heavily influenced by MACHINE stats. True **wearable armor** is rare, valuable, and usually district-flavored. These pieces act as powerful but optional layers on top of the chassis. This creates meaningful progression through both character investment and world exploration.

---

## 1. Layered Protection System

### Base Chassis Protection
Every player character begins with a foundational robotic chassis.

- **Base DT** = `floor( (Engine × 2.5) + (Might × 1.5) )`
- **Base DR** = `floor(Calculation × 2)` (as percentage)

Augmentations add flat bonuses or multipliers to these values.

### MACHINE Stat Modifiers
Stats improve how effectively your chassis and armor perform:

| Stat                  | Influence on Protection                                   | Mechanical Effect |
|-----------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------|-------------------|
| **Might (M)**         | Physical plating strength                                 | +1 DT per 2 points (Ballistic/Melee) |
| **Engine (E)**        | Core durability & heat/siligel management                 | +1 DT per point above 5; reduces degradation |
| **Calculation (C)**   | Energy dispersion & system optimization                   | +1% DR per point (Energy/Disrupt) |
| **Agility (A)**       | Positioning & partial evasion                             | Chance to reduce incoming DT effectiveness |
| **Nerve (N)**         | Stress & electronic warfare resistance                    | Reduces DT/DR penalties from Disrupt |
| **Investigation (I)** | Weak-point analysis                                       | Bonus to armor penetration when attacking |
| **Humanity (H)**      | Support & leadership                                      | Small DT/DR aura for companions |

---

## 2. Rare Wearable Armor Philosophy

- Wearable armor is **scarce** and feels like a meaningful upgrade.
- Pieces are layered **on top** of the chassis (they do not replace it).
- Most pieces come with trade-offs (energy drain, reduced mobility, reputation effects, etc.).
- Availability is strongly tied to district reputation and exploration.

## 3. District-by-District Armor & Augmentation Profiles

### Aries (Raw Survival / Power Core)
- **Style:** Heavy industrial plating, overclocked cores.
- **Strength:** Extremely high DT, especially Ballistic/Explosive.
- **Typical Pieces:** Core Guardian Plating, Overclocked Reactor Harness.
- **Augmentations:** Heat-resistant chassis mods, raw power boosters.
- **Drawback:** Often increases energy consumption and reduces finesse.

### Taurus (Residential / Stability)
- **Style:** Reinforced home-defense plating, bonded composite.
- **Strength:** Excellent sustained DT and environmental resistance.
- **Typical Pieces:** Taurus (TBN), Family-Bonded Shielding.
- **Augmentations:** Stability regulators, personal habitat integration.

### Cancer (Nurturing / Sanctuary)
- **Style:** Protective, life-support focused gear.
- **Strength:** Strong companion auras and trauma/psychological resistance.
- **Typical Pieces:** Sanctuary Warden Vest, Caretaker’s Embrace Plating.

### Leo (Cultural / Morale)
- **Style:** Performance-enhanced, visually striking armor.
- **Strength:** Social/distraction bonuses + moderate all-around protection.
- **Typical Pieces:** Spotlight Sovereign Harness, Glowweave Performance Suit.

### Virgo (Maintenance / Undergrid)
- **Style:** Highly repairable, modular infrastructure gear.
- **Strength:** Self-repairing systems and excellent long-term durability.
- **Typical Pieces:** Silent Guardian Rig, System Sovereign Plating.

### Capricorn (Industrial / Ambition)
- **Style:** Meritocratic, heavy-duty construction armor.
- **Strength:** High DT and crafting/construction synergy bonuses.

### Aquarius (Visionary / Experimental)
- **Style:** Cutting-edge, energy-dispersion prototypes.
- **Strength:** Very high DR (especially Energy/Disrupt), unique special effects.
- **Typical Pieces:** Prototype Pioneer Lattice, Visionary Equilibrium Vest.
- **Risk:** Occasional instability or random side effects.

### Gemini (Information / Communication)
- **Style:** Data-hardened, lightweight network-integrated suits.
- **Strength:** Strong Disrupt/EMP resistance and information warfare bonuses.

### Libra (Diplomatic / Balance)
- **Style:** Formal, concealed protection with negotiation aids.
- **Strength:** Balanced DT/DR + reputation and mediation bonuses.

### Scorpio (Psychological / Transformative)
- **Style:** Shadow/rebirth-themed adaptive armor.
- **Strength:** Powerful transformation abilities and psychological resistance.
- **Typical Pieces:** Reborn Shadow Shroud, Truth Unveiled Lattice.

### Sagittarius (Frontier / Exploration)
- **Style:** Rugged, cold-weather reinforced gear.
- **Strength:** Excellent mobility and environmental survival bonuses.

### Pisces (Black Market / Underworld)
- **Style:** Stealth-oriented, fluid, illicit modifications.
- **Strength:** Strong stealth and deception bonuses with flexible protection.
- **Typical Pieces:** Shadow Broker Cloak, Veil Walker Harness.

## 4. Re-Spec Interactions with Protection

Re-specing is painful and directly impacts survivability:

| Re-Spec Method                  | District     | Protection Changes                                      | Identity / Roleplay Cost |
|---------------------------------|--------------|---------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------|
| **Forge Rebuild**               | Aries        | Large +DT, possible DR loss                             | More machine-like, reduced Humanity |
| **Rebirth Ritual**              | Scorpio      | New shadow/transformation defenses, temporary instability | Major personality shift |
| **Experimental Lattice Swap**   | Aquarius     | High DR gains with random weaknesses                    | System instability risk |
| **Market Rebuild**              | Pisces       | Stealth/fluid armor bonuses, possible hidden penalties  | Criminal reputation effects |
| **Deep System Purge**           | Virgo        | Optimized chassis, risk of losing augmentations         | Emotional/identity sterilization |
| **Official Clean Slate**        | Calethina    | Balanced, controlled adjustments                        | Emotional distance from Calethina |

Rare armor compatibility can change after re-spec (some pieces may lose effectiveness or gain new synergies).

## District-Specific Re-Spec Methods & Protection Effects

| Re-Spec Method                  | District     | Effect on DT/DR & Armor                                                                 | Flavor / Identity Cost |
|---------------------------------|--------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------------------|
| **Forge Rebuild**               | Aries        | Significant +DT (especially Ballistic), possible loss of DR. Can overclock for temporary DT spikes. | Become more machine-like; lose some Humanity bonuses and emotional nuance. |
| **Rebirth Ritual**              | Scorpio      | Can gain strong Disrupt/psychological resistance or “shadow” defensive abilities. Risk of temporary DT/DR instability. | Major personality/identity shift. Armor may feel “alive” or unpredictable. |
| **Experimental Lattice Swap**   | Aquarius     | High potential +DR (Energy/Disrupt) with random side effects or new weaknesses. | Risk of mutations or system instability. Rare armor compatibility may change. |
| **Market Rebuild**              | Pisces       | Access to unique stealth-oriented or “fluid” armor bonuses. Possible hidden penalties. | Criminal branding and reputation shifts. Some districts may distrust you. |
| **Deep System Purge**           | Virgo        | Can reset or optimize chassis DT/DR. Risk of losing random augmentations or memories tied to protection. | Feel more “sterile” or machine-efficient; emotional range may decrease. |
| **Official Clean Slate**        | Calethina    | Controlled adjustment of DT/DR. Usually balanced but with emotional cost from Calethina. | She may become more distant; you feel “reset” in ways that affect roleplay. |

**Notes:**
- Re-specing directly alters Base DT/DR through changes to MACHINE stats.
- Some rare armor pieces may lose effectiveness or gain new synergies after certain re-specs.
- These methods tie into the heavy trade-off costs of the re-spec system.

## 5. Capstone Questline Rewards

Completing a district’s post-Idolized capstone quest often grants one of the strongest protective rewards available:

- **Permanent chassis upgrades** (e.g., +10 DT / +8% DR)
- **Signature rare armor piece** tied to the player’s final choice
- **Unique augmentations** only available through that district
- **District-wide passive bonuses** when wearing that district’s armor (e.g., Aries armor gives DT bonuses near power sources)

---

## 6. Design Notes & Integration Goals

- **Progression Feel:** Chassis + stats = reliable baseline. Rare armor = exciting power spikes.
- **District Identity:** Each district’s tech and culture is clearly reflected in the armor/augmentations they provide.
- **Player Agency:** Players can lean heavily into one or two districts for specialized protection or spread investment for versatility.
- **Re-Spec Weight:** Changing your MACHINE stats or undergoing district re-specs should feel like rewriting *how* you survive, not just who you are.
- **Neutral 13th District:** Late-game access to hybrid or master-tier armor that combines multiple district technologies.

This system reinforces the themes of scarcity, identity, and district loyalty while giving combat meaningful tactical depth.
